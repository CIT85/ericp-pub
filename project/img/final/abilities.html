Tactical Ability

Each character has a tactical ability that can be used more frequently than their other abilities. These abilities often provide tactical advantages in combat or support teammates in various ways.

Passive Ability

Passive abilities are innate traits possessed by each character. They usually grant passive bonuses or unique attributes that enhance the character's playstyle.

Ultimate Ability

Ultimate abilities are powerful abilities that charge over time or through specific actions during a match. Once charged, they can be unleashed to turn the tide of battle, dealing significant damage, providing strong utility, or offering valuable support to the team.
